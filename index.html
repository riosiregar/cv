About Me

Hi there. My name is Rio Ferdian Hidayat and I work as a multilingual translator and editor. I have had extensive experience in the field of translation since 2014, both as a freelancer and as a professional. The projects that I have tackled include, but not limited to, Japanese anime, feature films, comics, song lyrics, and business/corporate documents.

Services

Subtitle

Here I provide translation services for Japanese anime, live-action series, and feature films in the form of subtitles. The range of languages I provide is listed in the expertise section above. I emphasize fast work and guaranteed results. Deadlines and number of revisions will be based on initial consensus with the customer.

Video Editing

Provide personal video editing services, corporate promotional videos, or other types of videos without any conceptual restrictions (i.e censorship, slideshow, transition, etc). The work will be according to what the customer wants. Deadlines and rates will be based on the consensus discussed earlier.

Translation

Provide translation services for scientific papers, film/animation scripts, and corporate documents. All kinds of paperwork are included in this services category. The accuracy and results of the translation are guaranteed.
